Wellcome To Bangladesh

A nation of more than 160 million people, Bangladesh is nestled between India and Myanmar and is just a short flight away from most South Asian countries. With its variety of religious celebrations, a tapestry of high-rise apartments, bustling marketplaces peppered with parks and gardens, and a fascinating host of cuisines, the capital Dhaka has a lot to offer its guests. Despite its congestion and dense population, not unlike other cosmopolitan cities across the world, Dhaka holds a special place in the hearts of its residents.
